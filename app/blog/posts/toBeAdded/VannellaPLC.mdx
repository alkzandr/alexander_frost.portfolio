## Backplate design
<>
  <img src="/images/Backplate.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="400" height="300" />
</>

Backplate was designed with DIN rail and Panduit cable ducting for basic structure. Power supply located near bottom of PLC panel as it produces the most heat and this will encourage conduction.
